How do you calculate Clicks Per Second?

Click per second is nothing but calculating the frequency of your clicks. You can calculate your CPS by Dividing your Total Number of Clicks by the time you took for clicking.

Here's the formula you can use:

Clicks Per Second (CPS) = Total Number of Clicks / Total Time in Seconds

Let's go through an example to illustrate how to calculate CPS:

Example:

If you click 30 times in 5 seconds, what is your CPS?

Solution:

Total Number of Clicks = 30, Total Time in Seconds = 5

CPS = 30 / 5 = 6 CPS

In this example, if you clicked 30 times in a time span of 5 seconds, your CPS would be 6 clicks per second.
